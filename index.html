Live video, quietly behind your desktop.

Live Stream Wallpaper is a macOS menu-bar app for playing user-provided YouTube and HLS streams as a muted, non-interactive desktop backdrop.

Get support Read privacy policy

User-provided streams

Add HTTPS HLS playlist URLs or YouTube video URLs. The app rejects local files and non-HTTPS stream URLs.

Desktop-first behavior

The overlay ignores mouse events and pauses during screen saver activity, display sleep, Low Power Mode, or battery power when configured.

Private by default

Stream settings are stored locally on your Mac. The app does not collect, sell, or proxy your stream URLs.
