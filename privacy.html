Privacy Support

Privacy Policy

Live Stream Screensaver stores your stream list and preferences locally on your Mac. The app does not collect, sell, upload, or proxy your stream URLs.

Local data

The app stores stream URLs, selected stream state, and playback behavior preferences in local app storage so the app can restore your settings.

Third-party streams

If you enter a YouTube URL, YouTube content is loaded directly in an embedded web view and may be subject to YouTube and Google privacy practices. If you enter an HLS URL, your Mac connects directly to that stream provider.

Website

This website is hosted with GitHub Pages. It does not intentionally collect personal information or run analytics.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the support options on the support page.
