Support

For help with Live Stream Wallpaper, open an issue in the public support repository or use the support contact listed in the App Store.

Before reporting playback issues

  • Confirm the stream URL uses HTTPS.
  • For YouTube, check whether the video allows embedding.
  • For HLS, confirm the playlist URL opens outside the app.
  • Check whether Low Power Mode or battery pause settings are active.
